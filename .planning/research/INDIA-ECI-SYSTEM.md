# Research: Election Commission of India (ECI) System

This document summarizes the official election stages and regulatory framework for India, the primary destination country for VoteUp.

## 🇮🇳 India (Election Commission of India)
**System:** Parliamentary (FPTP)
**Primary Body:** Election Commission of India (ECI)

### **1. Official Election Pipeline Stages**
Based on ECI protocols, the VoteUp pipeline for India will follow these 8 stages:

1. **Pre-Election Preparations:** Electoral roll revision and constituency delimitation.
2. **Schedule Announcement:** MCC (Model Code of Conduct) takes effect immediately.
3. **Issue of Notification:** Formal legal notice by the President/Governor.
4. **Nomination & Scrutiny:** Candidates file papers; ECI verifies eligibility.
5. **Withdrawal Period:** Final date for candidates to step down; final list published.
6. **Campaign Period:** Ends 48 hours before the start of polling (Silence Period begins).
7. **Polling (Multi-phase):** Casting of votes via EVM/VVPAT.
8. **Counting & Declaration:** Centralized counting and formal result announcement.

### **2. Key Regulatory Features**
- **Model Code of Conduct (MCC):** A set of guidelines for political parties and candidates regarding speeches, polling day, polling booths, and election manifestos.
- **EVM & VVPAT:** Electronic Voting Machines and Voter Verifiable Paper Audit Trail. This is a unique technical aspect compared to many "Home" countries.
- **Multi-phase Polling:** Large-scale elections (Lok Sabha) happen in several phases (7 phases in 2024). VoteUp must handle phase-specific data for the user's constituency.

### **3. Citizen Action Layer Triggers**
- **Voter Registration:** Special emphasis on "Form 6" for new voters.
- **EPIC Card:** Verification of Electors Photo Identity Card.
- **Polling Booth Locator:** Finding the specific booth using "Know Your Polling Station".
- **Candidate Scrutiny:** Viewing the "Criminal Antecedents" and "Assets/Liabilities" of local candidates (via ECI's 'Know Your Candidate' portal).

## Comparison Matrix Highlights (India vs. Common Home Countries)
| Feature | India (ECI) | Common Contrast (e.g., Nigeria/US) |
|---|---|---|
| **Voting Tech** | EVM + VVPAT | Paper Ballots (US) / BVAS (Nigeria) |
| **Silence Period** | Strict 48 hours before poll | Varies (USA has no federal silence) |
| **Campaign Duration** | Typically ~2-3 weeks after withdrawal | Often much longer (90 days in Nigeria) |
| **MCC** | Highly regulated by ECI | Less centralized conduct rules in many systems |
