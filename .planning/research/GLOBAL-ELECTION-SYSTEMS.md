# Research: Comparative Election Systems (Anchor: India)

This document summarizes the election stages of common "Home" countries to provide comparison data for VoteUp, anchoring all comparisons against the **Indian (ECI)** system.

## 🇨🇦 Canada (Comparison)
**Anchor Contrast:** Canada's "Writ Drop" is the primary trigger, similar to India's "Issue of Notification". Both follow parliamentary systems but differ in campaign duration (Canada: 36-50 days vs. India: ~3 weeks).

## 🇳🇬 Nigeria (Comparison)
**Anchor Contrast:** Nigeria has a significantly longer campaign period (90 days) compared to India. Both have strict silence periods, but Nigeria uses BVAS technology while India uses EVM/VVPAT.

## 🇬🇧 United Kingdom (Comparison)
**Anchor Contrast:** Shared colonial DNA with India, but the UK's timetable is much tighter (25 working days from start to finish). India's multi-phase approach is unique compared to the UK's single-day poll.

## 🇺🇸 United States (Comparison)
**Anchor Contrast:** Dramatic difference in voting tech (Paper/Optical Scan vs. India's EVM). The US has a prolonged primary season which India does not have at the national level.
